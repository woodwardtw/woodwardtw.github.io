I am passionate about education and have spent the last fifteen years focused on blending constructivism and technology in K12 and higher ed. My experience includes private and public institutions and non-profits. My interests are varied and include multimedia creation, data visualization, history, APIs, poetry, WordPress, and photography.

This portfolio is an attempt to paint a real picture of what I can do and show actual work backing up the things I say. Some of that work is episodic, and some is long-term, but it's all evidence of patterns of behavior that reflect my core beliefs about education and learning.

This site was built not just to represent me and my work but also to explore how I could use various APIs, Angular, and JavaScript to create an interactive experience where the content is generated through other tools and platforms.
